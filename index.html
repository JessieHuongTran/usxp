Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about expanding your business into the United States.

How long does U.S. expansion take?+
Most clients are operational in the U.S. within 60 to 90 days. Forming a legal entity typically takes 2 to 4 weeks depending on structure and state. A full market entry, including setup, compliance, hiring, and first customers, usually takes 3 to 6 months depending on your industry, readiness, and complexity.
Do I need to relocate to the U.S.?+
Not necessarily. Many founders successfully build and run U.S. operations remotely or with a hybrid setup. In some cases, particularly for go-to-market execution, enterprise sales, or visa requirements, having a U.S.-based founder or executive can significantly accelerate progress.
What industries do you support?+
We support a wide range of industries. We have direct experience in agriculture, textiles and apparel, furniture and home goods, seafood and aquaculture, processed foods, manufacturing, industrial components, packaging, and consumer goods supply chains.
