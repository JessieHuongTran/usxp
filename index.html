Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about expanding your business into the United States.

How long does U.S. expansion take?+
Most of our clients are operationally ready in the U.S. within 60–90 days. Legal entity formation typically takes 2–4 weeks. Full market entry — including team, office, and first customers — typically takes 3–6 months depending on your industry and complexity.
Do I need to relocate to the U.S.?+
Not necessarily. Many of our clients successfully operate U.S. entities with a remote or hybrid team. However, for certain visa types and business models, having a U.S.-based founder or executive can significantly accelerate growth.
What industries do you support?+
We have deep expertise in SaaS, AI/ML, fintech, e-commerce, deep tech, health tech, and consumer brands. Our playbooks are industry-specific. If your industry isn't listed, contact us — we likely have relevant experience.
Do you help with fundraising or investors?+
Yes. We have a curated network of U.S. VCs, angel investors, and family offices. We can make warm introductions and help you prepare your U.S. investor narrative, pitch deck, and cap table structure.
